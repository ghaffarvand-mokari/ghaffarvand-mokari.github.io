About

I am a postdoctoral researcher currently affiliated with the University of Eastern Finland. My work focuses on phonetics, speech perception, and second-language speech learning. I am particularly interested in how listeners perceive variation in speech, how speech perception and production are connected, and how cognitive factors interact with language learning.

Outside of research, I enjoy playing the tar and guitar, cross-country skiing, and flight simulation.
